Lighting Design

The process of crafting a new design brings me new problems to solve and new stories to tell. With a Design Contract you will get a fully realized Lighting Design that supports the other aspects of your work, while maintaining a strong voice in and of itself.

Along with the physical manifestation of the design, you will also receive copies of all the paperwork, and details that go into creating it, for your records.

Assistant Lighting Design

As an Assistant Lighting Designer I bring many skills to the table, including Drafting, Communication Skills, Paperwork Management Skills, Crew Leadership Experience, and a Designers sensibility. Having worked as an Assistant in places ranging from Small/Medium Ballet Companies, to LORT Theaters, I can help set you up for success in realizing your ideas of a Lighting Design.
